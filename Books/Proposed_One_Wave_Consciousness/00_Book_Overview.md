# PROPOSED ONE-WAVE CONSCIOUSNESS
## Active Hypothesis Book — Overview

Status: GREEN hypothesis / YELLOW grounding and mathematics

This book is an active One-Wave research section. It is not a claim that consciousness has been solved, anatomically located, or experimentally reproduced.

The purpose is to preserve and test the full consciousness architecture without confusing three different statements:

1. **One-Wave hypothesis:** consciousness may arise from bounded recursive feedback, scale-aware modulation, memory, choice, and synchronized access.
2. **Biological comparison:** nervous systems may contain functional parallels to that architecture.
3. **Engineering proposal:** an artificial system can be built with analogous functions and tested without claiming that the build is conscious.

Unknown status is not a reason to remove the work. Every chapter must state what is:

- proposed,
- inherited from an accepted One-Wave node,
- analogous to established biology,
- mathematically derived,
- simulated,
- built,
- measured,
- or unresolved.

## Core Working Architecture

The current hypothesis studies three functional layers:

- **Generative / expressive layer** — forms possibilities, simulations, associations, and candidate movements.
- **M4 scale-weighing / mirror layer** — compares urgency, intensity, compatibility, risk, cost, restraint, and drive without independently committing action.
- **Administrative / compressive layer** — maintains identity, reference state, memory, permissions, resources, continuity, and responsibility for commitment.

Working names such as Four Mind, Five Mind, Six Mind, Dream Engine, M4, and Administrator are allowed here as hypothesis labels. They must not be presented as settled anatomical entities or proven seats of awareness.

- Can bounded recursive feedback produce a persistent self-model?

- Does M4-like scale weighing explain the transition between fast impulse, restraint, deliberate choice, and shutdown?
- Does loop release require synchronization between a completed state report and acceptance of commitment?
- Can the same architecture recur at cell, neural, individual, relational, group, and machine scales?
- Which similarities are scale recurrence, architectural homology, or actual mathematical scale invariance?
- What observations would falsify the architecture?

## Proof Boundary

Nothing in this book is promoted merely because it is coherent. Coherence permits investigation. Derivation, simulation, implementation, and measurement determine promotion.
