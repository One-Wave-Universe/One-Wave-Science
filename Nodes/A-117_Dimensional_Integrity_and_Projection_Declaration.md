# Node A-117: Dimensional Integrity and Projection Declaration

**Dependencies**  
Upstream: A-101 Ground / Zero, A-102 Displacement, A-103 Differential, A-113 Projection, A-116 Three-Dimensional Spherical Default  
Downstream: D-408 Sixfold 2D Lattice, D-409 Twelvefold 3D Coordination, D-410 Twenty-Fourfold 4D Recurrence Shell, all physical simulations, all geometric wiki pages

## Purpose

A One-Wave structure must be defined in its native dimensional layer before it is drawn, projected, simulated, or compared with another layer.

The canonical rule is

\[
\boxed{2D\neq3D\neq4D}
\]

and

\[
\boxed{6{:}1\neq12{:}1\neq24{:}1}.
\]

These layers may be related by explicit projection, embedding, recurrence, or scale operators. They may not be silently collapsed into one mechanism.

## Native Dimensional Layers

### Two-dimensional layer

The 2D native geometry is the sixfold triangular/hexagonal lattice. A center has six in-plane nearest neighbors, producing the local coordination relation

\[
6{:}1.
\]

Its canonical local cluster is one center plus six surrounding cells or sites. This layer is defined in D-408.

### Three-dimensional layer

The 3D native geometry is volumetric. The current canonical lattice candidate is close-packed coordination, where one center has twelve nearest neighbors:

\[
12{:}1.
\]

Bounded physical modes remain subject to A-116's spherical or sphere-like minimum-surface default. This layer is defined in D-409.

### Four-dimensional layer

The 4D layer is the ordered recurrence of a complete 3D state through time, phase, sequence, and Mirror-Gate crossings. Unless a node explicitly derives a fourth spatial coordinate, 4D does **not** mean a second hidden volume added to the three spatial dimensions.

The canonical coordination relation is

\[
24{:}1,
\]

meaning twenty-four coordinated Field/Void or expression/compression positions around one persistent identity/reference through a completed recurrence shell. It is not automatically a claim of twenty-four spatial nearest neighbors. This layer is defined in D-410.

## Relationship Between the Ratios

The ratios are nested but not interchangeable. The same numeric notation may also appear in neural fan-in, oversight, throttling, scale, or conversion grammar. A matching number does not prove a matching mechanism. Every use must name its domain.

The ratios are nested but not interchangeable:

\[
6{:}1\quad\text{local 2D adjacency},
\]

\[
12{:}1\quad\text{local 3D volumetric adjacency},
\]

\[
24{:}1\quad\text{completed 4D recurrence coordination}.
\]

The architecture may be written schematically as

\[
6{:}1\;\xrightarrow{\text{3D embedding}}\;12{:}1
\;\xrightarrow{\text{Field/Void recurrence}}\;24{:}1,
\]

but neither arrow is a proof that one ratio numerically generates the next. Each transition requires its own geometry or recurrence operator.

## Projection Rule

Let \(X_D\) be a native state in dimension \(D\), and let

\[
P_{D\rightarrow d}:X_D\rightarrow X_d,
\qquad d<D,
\]

be a declared projection.

A projected quantity \(Q\) is valid only when either

\[
Q(X_D)=Q_d(P_{D\rightarrow d}X_D)
\]

for an invariant quantity, or the projection error is explicitly measured:

\[
\epsilon_Q
=
\left|Q(X_D)-Q_d(P_{D\rightarrow d}X_D)\right|.
\]

A visually accurate projection is not automatically dynamically accurate.

## Simulation Rule

A 2D simulation may test sixfold planar transfer, cell-area change, ring circulation, and overlap geometry. It may not claim to have derived a complete 3D knot.

A 3D simulation may test volumetric recurrence, knot geometry, internal/external shell relations, and twelvefold local coordination. It may not claim a complete 4D recurrence from one static frame.

A 4D simulation must preserve the ordered history of the evolving 3D state, including phase, Mirror crossings, direction, return path, and recurrence identity.

## Failure Conditions

This node fails if:

1. a lower-dimensional representation reproduces every native higher-dimensional degree of freedom without loss and an explicit isomorphism is derived;
2. the canonical 6:1 or 12:1 coordination is replaced by a different native geometry that performs better under the repository's tests;
3. 24:1 is physically derived as a different structure than the completed Field/Void recurrence shell.

Until one of those results exists, dimensional separation is mandatory.
