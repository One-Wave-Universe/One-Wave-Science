## REVISED — v1 collapsed a real scale distinction

### 8. Meet-in-the-Middle Sync
**v1:** Duplicate (E-524 Kuramoto Lattice Synchronization).
**Problem with v1:** E-524 is explicitly scoped to lattice oscillators — a specific scale and substrate. The packet's claim was broader: that the same synchronization pattern applies to hemispheres, relationships, and teams, not just lattice cells.
**v2 disposition:** E-524 remains the correct **Scale-Specific Instance** at the lattice scale — no change there. The packet's broader claim is **Scale Recurrence, candidate only** — the same ordered pattern (compare phase/speed/intensity → adjust → lock) plausibly recurs at other scales, but no variable correspondence table exists yet between Kuramoto's θᵢ/ωᵢ and anything at the relational or organizational scale. **Action:** do not merge into E-524. Open a cross-scale comparison table (I-04 §Required Procedure) with E-524 as the lattice instance and the general "meet-in-the-middle" pattern as the parent, before deciding whether relational/team instances belong as siblings.

### 10. 12:1 Maximum Throttle
**v1:** Unproven Delta.
**Refinement, not reversal:** this is a cleaner example of **Candidate Scale Invariance** specifically, not a generic unproven claim. The packet asserts the *same numeric ceiling* holds at every scale — that's a stronger, more falsifiable claim than a vague delta, and I-04's evidence ladder gives it a precise test: define the rescaling (what is x → λx here?), show 12:1 survives it. C-309's γ_max/γ_min and C-312's 3:1 are both candidates for what the "invariant quantity" would even be, and they disagree with each other, let alone with 12. **Action:** unchanged filing status (not promoted), but reclassify as Candidate Scale Invariance so future work targets the actual required evidence (variable correspondence, defined rescaling) rather than generic "needs derivation."
