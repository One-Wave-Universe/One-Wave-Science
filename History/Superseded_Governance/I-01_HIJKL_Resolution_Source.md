## Addendum to I-01: H/I/J/K/L Appendix Proposal — Resolution Record

Status: Resolution complete for mapping; new content built separately (see E-517)

This is a worked example of I-01 Rule 4 (Same Word Does Not Mean Same
Concept) and Rule 5 (Citation Titles Reliable, Numbers Often Not) —
applied here to a whole proposed appendix structure rather than a
single node.

A five-appendix proposal (H, I, J, K, L) was checked against the real
repo. Result: roughly two-thirds duplicated existing nodes under new
addresses, one letter (I) directly collided with the already-
established Special Rules series, and a small number of items were
genuinely new. None of H/I/J/K/L were adopted as new appendices.
