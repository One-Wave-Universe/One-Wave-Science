## Fibonacci-word convention

```text
W0 = 0
W1 = 01
Wk = W(k-1) W(k-2)
```

Fibonacci token `0` is not the Mirror Gate. Sign mirroring preserves the token trace. Reverse traversal compares against the reversed expected trace and does not imply token complement.

## Golden-ratio scope

The golden ratio is checked through the ordered Fibonacci-word consequences:

- generation-length ratios;
- zero-to-one symbol-count ratios;
- exact recursive concatenation;
- balance and factor-complexity checks.

It does not validate the sixfold lattice, seven-cell cluster, Flower-of-Life or Metatron overlays, `6:1`, `12:1`, `24:1`, quark physics, proton physics, or Mass Effect.
