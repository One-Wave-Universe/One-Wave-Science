Definition

A four-level control architecture for a physical body: Local Sensors (autonomous, no neighbor awareness) -> Local Aggregation (3:1 compression) -> Positional Chains -> Central Coordinator (modulates, does not dictate).

Mathematics

3:1 fan-in compression, repeating at every level. Coordinator samples at half the base signaling frequency (3 base cycles per 1 oversight cycle). Correction is targeted, not broadcast.
Signal count compressing 3:1 at each level from sensors up to the coordinator -- the concrete, buildable half of this node's design.

Math Trail (dependency chain)

Explicitly and repeatedly declines to claim this hierarchy is a seat of awareness -- its own Boundary Statement states this framing 'was declined and stays declined,' a deliberate limit that G-718 and G-719 both cite directly when disambiguating their own consciousness-adjacent material from this one.

Recursive Reframing

The fan-in/fan-out structure is already a genuine recursive compression (3 inputs to 1 output, repeating at every level) -- one of the more concretely engineered recursive structures in the entire repository, not a proposal.

Yellow Audit — what's still open

Position-encoding scheme (the 'hex-L' proposal) has no formal definition. Command latency through four levels is undefined. Whether 4 levels is the right number, vs. flattened or expanded, is untested.
