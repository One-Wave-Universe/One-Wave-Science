Definition

The return tendency of a displaced medium toward equilibrium -- the most basic stability mechanism.

Mathematics

V_f(ψ)=½K_fψ². R_f=−dV_f/dψ=−K_fψ. Response always points back toward ψ=0 (sign-checked both directions).
The restoring potential -- a simple parabola, with stiffness K_f still unmeasured.

Math Trail

The simplest of the four stability mechanisms E-506 combines (Flowback, Pressure, Surface, Coupling) -- a clean, textbook restoring-force form.

Recursive Reframing

Static potential; recursive form would track ψⁿ⁺¹=ψⁿ+R_f(ψⁿ)·Δt, folding directly into the core update rule.

Yellow Audit

K_f (flowback stiffness) unknown until measurement. Whether constant or displacement-dependent unresolved. Relationship to A-105's operator form A not yet formalized.
