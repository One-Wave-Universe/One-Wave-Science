Definition

The boundary region where one displacement state meets another. Surface energy resists unnecessary boundary growth -- stable modes tend toward minimum-surface (spherical) geometry.

Mathematics

E_s=σA_s. Spherical case: A_s=4πR², so E_s=4πσR².
Surface energy rising with R-squared -- the geometric reason stable modes favor spherical shapes.

Math Trail
