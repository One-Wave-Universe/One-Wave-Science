Definition

Reciprocal redistribution of a bounded quantity between two states. The total is conserved: what one side gains, the other loses.

Mathematics

Closure: Q_total = Q₁ + Q₂ = constant dQ₁/dt = −dQ₂/dt ΔQ₁ + ΔQ₂ = 0
Q_1(t) and Q_2(t) trading places while their sum (gray dotted) stays constant — the defining signature of Transfer.

Math Trail (dependency chain)

Built directly on F-602's differential (a nonzero D_ψ is what drives the redistribution) and feeds three separate downstream nodes (Resonance, Interference, Attenuation) plus one node (F-609 Amplification) that does not yet exist as a file — a real gap, already logged in DEPENDENCY_FLOW_AUDIT.md.

Recursive Reframing

This is already naturally recursive in spirit (a conservation law under continuous exchange), but the node states it as a continuous derivative. The discrete recursive form used elsewhere in this repo would be: Q₁n+1 = Q₁n + ΔQ, Q₂n+1 = Q₂n − ΔQ, which is exactly the per-step bookkeeping the core update rule already uses elsewhere — worth stating explicitly here rather than leaving Transfer as the one F-node still written in continuous-time language.
