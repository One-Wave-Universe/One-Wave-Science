Definition

The local state generated by imbalance within the field. Pressure is a field quantity, not a surface quantity, and does not itself describe motion.

Mathematics

P = f(B) B=0, f(0)=0 ⇒ P=0 B≠0 ⇒ P=f(B) (constitutive form of f unspecified)

Math Trail (dependency chain)
