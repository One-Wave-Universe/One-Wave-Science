Definition

Mathematics

V(x) = ∫A(s)ds from 0 to x. Linear case: A(x)=αx ⇒ V(x)=½αx²
Potential energy (middle panel), the same quadratic well shape as classical harmonic potential, built from A-105's restoring response.

Math Trail (dependency chain)

Directly built on A-105's restoring-response operator — potential energy is literally the stored integral of that response, so this node's Yellow status is entirely inherited, not independent.

Recursive Reframing

The integral form is naturally a running-sum recursion: V_{n+1}=V_n+A(x_n)·Δx, accumulating step by step rather than evaluated as one closed-form integral.
