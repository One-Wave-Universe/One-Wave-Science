Definition

A persistent mode whose recursive update returns to itself after k steps -- a stable periodic pattern.

Mathematics

ψ_(n+k) = ψ_n, integer k≥1. k=1: fixed point. k=2: period-2. k=n: period-n resonance. Condition: ||ψ_(n+k)−ψ_n|| < ε AND periodic.
Three periods (k=1 fixed point, k=2, k=4) shown as discrete stem plots -- the defining psi_(n+k)=psi_n condition at different k.
