Definition

A bidirectional wavegate operating between two reference positions through a shared middle -- every exchange begins at one reference, crosses the shared middle, reaches the paired reference, and returns. RENAMED this project from an earlier 'Mirror Gate' title that collided directly with C-301 Mirror Gate.

Base grammar: A(0)B, B(0)A. Fundamental pair: 1(0)-1, -1(0)1. Working progression: 1(0)-1 through 5(0)-5 (magnitude increasing each step).
